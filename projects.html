Meetings and Projects

Meetings:

  • Held monthly (2nd Saturday, 4:00–5:00 p.m.)
  • Rotational hosting or as agreed
  • Notice via WhatsApp reminders

Quorum: 2/3 or 75% of members

Records: All minutes must be signed by the Chairperson and at least one committee member to confirm accuracy.

Community Projects

The association prioritizes projects that enhance social well-being, environmental conservation, and local development.
