Membership

Eligibility

  • Resident within Kaetoni River Drive boundaries
  • Aged 18 years and above
  • Of good conduct

Joining Process

  • Complete membership form
  • Pay Ksh 2,000 registration + 6 months’ contribution
  • Approval by two-thirds of members

Rights

  • Vote and be elected
  • Access information and services
  • Participate in meetings and decisions

Obligations

  • Attend meetings (penalties apply for absence or lateness)
  • Pay monthly contributions (Ksh 1,000)
  • Support association projects
