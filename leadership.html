Leadership and Governance

Management Committee: 5–7 members, elected for a 3-year term, eligible for a maximum of two consecutive terms.

Key Officials

  • Chairperson: Leads meetings, represents association
  • Deputy Chair: Acts in Chair’s absence
  • Secretary: Keeps minutes and issues notices
  • Treasurer: Manages finances and maintains records

Elections Timeline

  • Held every 3 years from the date of inception.
  • Members eligible for election must have served at least 6 months actively.
  • Election process conducted during a scheduled general meeting.

Amendments

Changes to by-laws require approval by two-thirds of the members.

Dissolution

Can occur through a formal resolution by members during an official meeting.
